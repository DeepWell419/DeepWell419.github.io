Empowering kids to navigate life.

Adventure Bound has been designed to facilitate the passing on of skills and values to kids at a formational time in life. Achievement-based, this program is meant to create an environment where kids can safely confront their limitations, learn to process difficulty and disappointment, as well as develop resolve and perseverance. Faith-centered, our activities will support the normalization and integration of faith into everyday life.

You may be wondering...

What age(s) is this for?

Currently, we are able to accommodate kids ages 6-12 years old.

What happens at an Adventure Bound meeting?

Each meeting will be composed of a few different elements:
     - A time of Bible-based teaching, connecting a message of faith to our lives & the skill we are developing.
     - Demonstration and training on a life skill and its practical application.
     - Play time and a snack!

How much does Adventure Bound cost?

Adventure Bound is free to register with the only cost being $15 to cover badges & a vest to wear them on.  

Optional expenses in the future would include any retreats/ camping trips taken.

Can we check this out before we register?

We would ask that you register first. Because we meet in homes, we refrain from posting meeting locations online. 

Registration in no way obligates you to join and mostly serves as a way for us to know how many people to plan for at our next meeting (for snacks, crafts, etc.).

Can kids come without an adult?

Kids are welcome to be dropped off at a meeting and picked up at the end.
However, adults will be counted on to help their child practice honing their skills at home.
